About

Social Game Tycoon is a game created by Arne Ubelhor as part of a dissertation project for the University of Lincoln. The game was created in order to better understand and comprehend the design of social games.


For the purposes of the study I will be collecting data anonymously of how you interact with the game world and any opinions I survey, best efforts will be made to not make the data identifiable.
